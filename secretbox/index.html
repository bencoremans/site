🔐 SecretBox

Secure AES-256-GCM encryption in your browser - nothing leaves your device

Credential mode: Encrypt usernames and passwords with labels for easy organization.
Free text mode: Encrypt any text, JSON, configurations, or large secrets.
Decrypt: Paste your encrypted text and master key to reveal the original content.

100% Privacy: All encryption uses Web Crypto API (AES-256-GCM). Nothing is sent to any server.
